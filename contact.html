FAQs

Common Questions

Everything you need to know before your first session.

How does a virtual session work?

You'll join via a secure video link. Your physio will assess your condition visually, guide you through exercises in real-time, and monitor your form. It's as effective as an in-clinic session for most conditions.

What equipment do I need?

Just a smartphone, tablet or laptop with a camera, and a clear space to move. For some programmes, your physio may recommend simple home equipment like resistance bands or a mat.

How long is each session?

Initial assessments are typically 45–60 minutes. Follow-up treatment sessions are 30–45 minutes depending on your programme.

Is virtual physio covered by insurance?

Many insurance providers in India now cover telehealth physiotherapy. We provide detailed receipts and reports to support your claims. Please check with your insurer for specific coverage details.

Can I cancel or reschedule?

Yes. We require at least 12 hours' notice for cancellations or rescheduling. You can manage your appointments through our platform or by contacting our support team directly.
