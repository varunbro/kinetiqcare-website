Our Story

Physio Without Barriers

Kinetiq Care was born from a simple frustration: too many people in India were missing out on quality physiotherapy simply because they couldn't get to a clinic. Long commutes, limited availability, and stigma around rehabilitation were keeping people in pain.

We built Kinetiq Care to change that. By harnessing technology and pairing it with genuinely qualified, experienced physiotherapists, we've created a platform that delivers clinical-grade care directly to your home, office, or wherever you are.

Today, we've helped over 2,500 patients across India recover, rehabilitate, and thrive — and we're just getting started.
